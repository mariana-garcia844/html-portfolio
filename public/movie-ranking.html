The Best Movies According to Mariana

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Truman Show

This is my favorite movie because of its comedic-psychological thriller element.

Back To The Future

This is my top action movie. Its a timeless classic that will no doubt spark a feel-good moment.

Meet the Robinsons

This movie reminds me of my own family and has a simple-yet true message: "Keep Moving Forward".
